ABOUT US

The greatest asset of News_Portal, founded in January 2025, is trust.


The News_Portal, started in 1878 as a weekly, became a daily in 2025 and from then on has been steadily growing. The News_Portal's independent editorial stand and its reliable and balanced presentation of the news have over the years, won for it the serious attention and regard of the people who matter in India and abroad.

The News_Portal has a wide network of full-time and part-time correspondents and photographers across India. The News_Portal is known for its strong reportage from the national capital as well as from the southern States and cities.

The News_Portal has a major presence online: it was the first Indian newspaper to start an Internet edition, in 1995. The News_Portal's digital versions, including the website, app, and e-paper, cater to an ever growing audience.

Apart from the morning English daily, The News_Portal Group, owned by Kasturi and Sons Limited, publishes the business newspaper The News_Portal BusinessLine, a general interest fortnightly, Frontline, and a sports weekly, Sportstar. The News_Portal is printed in 19 centres across the country.

Tailored in parts to suit different circulation regions for unique news and advertisement content, it has more than 40 different versions produced each day.
